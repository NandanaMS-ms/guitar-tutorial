C Major Chord

The C major chord is played by pressing the following frets:

  • First finger: first fret of the second string.
  • Second finger: Second fret of the fourth string.
  • Third finger: Third fret of the fifth string.
  • Strum all strings.

C Minor Chord

The C minor chord is played by pressing the following frets:

  • First finger: fifth string at the third fret.Flatten your finger and press down to make a barre across to the first string.
  • Second finger: fourth fret of the second string.
  • Third finger: fifth fret of the fourth string.
  • Fourth finger: fifth fret of the third string.
  • Strum only the thinnest five strings.
